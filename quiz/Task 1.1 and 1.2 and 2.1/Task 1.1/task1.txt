**Functional Requirements for Contact Management Application**

1. **Add New Contact**: Users should be able to input and save new contacts with their first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.
  
2. **Update Contact**: Allow users to modify existing contacts by editing any of the contact's fields, including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

3. **Delete Contact**: Users should have the ability to delete contacts from the system.

4. **Search Contacts**: Provide a search functionality allowing users to find contacts by first name, last name, company, job title, phone number, or email address.

5. **Merge Duplicate Contacts**: Implement a feature to identify and merge duplicate contacts based on matching first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, or email addresses.

6. **View Contact Details**: Users should be able to view all details of a contact, including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

7. **Handle Multiple Phone Numbers and Email Addresses**: Allow contacts to have zero or more phone numbers and email addresses, with labels to distinguish them (e.g., home, work, mobile).

8. **Label Phone Numbers and Email Addresses**: Enable users to label phone numbers and email addresses to indicate their purpose or relation to the contact (e.g., home, work, mobile).

9. **Data Integrity**: Ensure the system maintains data integrity by validating inputs and preventing inconsistent or incorrect data entries.

10. **User Authentication and Authorization**: Implement user authentication and authorization to ensure only authorized users can access and manipulate contacts data.

11. **Data Persistence**: Store contact information persistently so that it remains available across sessions and system restarts.

12. **User-Friendly Interface**: Design an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for users to interact with the contact management application effectively.

13. **Data Backup and Recovery**: Provide mechanisms for data backup and recovery to prevent data loss in case of system failures or accidental deletions.

14. **Import and Export Contacts**: Allow users to import contacts from external sources and export contacts to share or backup the data.

15. **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Ensure the application is compatible with different operating systems and devices for seamless usage across platforms.
